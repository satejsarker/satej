Car Rentals

At Kempegowda International Airport Bengaluru, you also have the option of renting premium cars apart from airport taxis.
The rental services are provided by Carzonrent and Akbar Travels.
You can use the Limousine service from a fleet of high-end luxury cars to and from the airport.
An approximate rate for standard premium cars like Innova or Honda City is Rs.1,200 for distances up to 35 kilometers. You can pay either by cash or use a credit/ debit card.
For more information you can contact the airport counters of the service provider, located within the terminal building.
